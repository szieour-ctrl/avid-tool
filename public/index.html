How to use AVID Assist™
1
Start your Otter recording
Before walking the property, state these 6 lines clearly:
Broker firm is: [Firm Name]
Inspector is: [Your Name]
Date is: [Today's Date]
Time is: [Current Time]
Weather is: [Conditions]
Others present are: [Names or None]
2
Announce each room
Say the room name before describing it. Use exact AVID names:
I am now in the Living Room...
I am now in Bedroom 1...
I am now in Bath 1...
I am now at the Exterior...
3
Download AVID from Lone Wolf
Open the transaction in zipForm. Download the AVID PDF — address is pre-filled. Then open it, press Ctrl+P → Save as PDF to strip encryption.
4
Paste transcript & upload PDF
Copy your full Otter transcript and paste it in the left box. Upload your saved AVID PDF in the upload area below it.
5
Generate & download
Click Generate & Fill AVID. In 20–30 seconds a filled PDF will be ready to download. Review all fields before signing.
6
Review, sign & send
Open the filled PDF. Add any missing items flagged in red. Sign and send to transaction coordinator.
